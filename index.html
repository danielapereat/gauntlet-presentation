What We Built

🛡️

TommyGuardian — New Features

Smarter incident management with proactive monitoring and self-healing capabilities.

  • Watch deploys as they happen and catch issues early
  • Resolve incidents faster with new AI-powered skills
  • Integrated QA testing — both manual and automated
  • Fix CI/CD failures and comment on PRs automatically
  • Test changes in staging before going live
  • Deploy straight from Slack with a single command
🧪

QA MCP — Testing Made Simple

One tool to run any test — manual or automated — across all integrations.

  • Built for Integrations, expanding company-wide
  • Automate QA for any integration in minutes
  • Skip the Postman setup — just describe what to test
  • Essential for faster, more reliable on-call response

What We Built

💥

Chaos Engineering Agent

Find problems before they find us. An AI that stress-tests our systems so we're always ready.

  • Predict what could go wrong before it happens
  • Safely test failure scenarios in controlled environments
  • Identify weak points by generating chaos experiments
  • Shift from firefighting to proactive prevention
  • Propose chaos experiments based on past incidents (Postmortems)
🏢

Yuno 360 Agent

Everything you need to know about a merchant, in one place.

  • Know everything we have talked about with a merchant
  • Gong — Understand calls and identify decisions that affect our merchants
  • Slack — Surface relevant merchant conversations and context from internal channels
  • Track what the merchant has raised across internal channels and what we've discussed with them

Future Enhancements

  • Understand the Yuno-related team assigned to each merchant
  • Open Pylon and Jira tickets for the merchant
